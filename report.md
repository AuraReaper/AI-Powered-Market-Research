## Google Business Overview
Google operates in the technology industry, specifically in the internet, cloud computing, computer software, computer hardware, artificial intelligence, and advertising sectors. The company's market segment includes a wide range of products and services, such as Google Search, Google Maps, YouTube, Google Chrome, Gmail, Google Drive, and Google Cloud.

## Top 3 Competitors and Their AI/ML Benefits
The top 3 competitors of Google are Microsoft, Meta, and Amazon. Microsoft competes with Google in various sectors such as search engines, cloud computing, productivity software, and advertising. Meta competes with Google in the social media and online advertising spaces. Amazon competes with Google in the e-commerce and cloud computing spaces.
