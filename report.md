# Netflix
## Business Description and Top Competitors
Netflix operates in the entertainment and media industry, providing streaming services for TV series, documentaries, feature films, and games across various genres and languages. The company's target market includes a global audience in 190 countries, with a focus on younger adults from Gen Z and Millennials, and a lower income range of $25-50K. Netflix's key offerings include its extensive content library, personalized recommendations, and user-friendly interface. The company's strategic initiatives focus on expanding its content offerings, improving its user experience, and exploring new technologies such as virtual reality and artificial intelligence.

The top 3 competitors of Netflix are:
* Amazon Prime Video
* Hulu
* Disney+
