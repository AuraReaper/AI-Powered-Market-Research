Tesla's Business Overview

Tesla operates primarily within the automotive industry, focusing on electric vehicles (EVs), battery energy storage systems, solar panels, and related products. Its key offerings include a range of EVs, energy generation and storage solutions, autonomous driving technology (Autopilot and Full Self-Driving), and the Supercharger network. Tesla's strategic focus areas include technological innovation, global expansion, sustainable energy solutions, and software and data.
