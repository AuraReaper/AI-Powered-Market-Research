Google Overview

Google operates primarily within the technology industry, specifically in online advertising, cloud computing, search engine, operating systems, hardware, software & applications, and artificial intelligence. They target a vast global market encompassing individuals, businesses, developers, and governments. Google's overarching vision is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. Its product positioning emphasizes user-friendliness, innovation, and accessibility. Key offerings include Google Search, Google Ads, Google Cloud Platform (GCP), Android, and YouTube.
